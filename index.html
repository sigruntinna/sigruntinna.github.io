Potato Wars

Sigrún and Unnur are two farmers that both dream of winning the potato competition and controlling the market price of potatoes, lowering it until the other farmer goes out of business. But they have to grow their crops to succeed.

They can blow potatoes out of their cannons into each others garden to ruin for the other. They can also blow potatoes at flying objects such as birds that carry potatoes in a bag and drones and balloons that either carries water to give the potatoes a growth boost or a shovel to harvest the potato crop.

Polar Bear

This is a game about a little polar bear that is swimming in an ocean full of plastic bags and sharp ice!

The goal is to eat a lot of seals, watch out for toxic plastic bags and not hit the ice because it is sharp.
